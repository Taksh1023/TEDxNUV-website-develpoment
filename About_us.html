What is TED?


TED (Technology, Entertainment, Design) is a global platform that brings people together to share ideas and stories worth spreading. Started in 1984 as a conference focused on technology and design, TED has since evolved to cover a wide range of topics from science to business to global issues. The goal of TED is to inspire and empower people to think critically, explore new ideas, and make positive change in the world.

What is TEDx?


TEDx is a local, independently organized version of TED, created to bring the spirit of TED to communities around the world. TEDx events are organized by passionate individuals who are passionate about spreading ideas and creating impact in their communities. The format of TEDx events is similar to TED, with talks and performances given by local speakers and performers. The goal of TEDx is to bring people together to share ideas, spark conversations, and make a positive impact in their communities.

What is TEDxNuv?


TEDx NUV shares the same passion as TED. We are here to spread your ideas all across the globe. Our objective is to reach out and inspire people with “Ideas Worth Sharing” which has the potential to help people make a strong difference in their lives. TEDx NUV is oriented at developing a community of like-minded highly informed and engaged innovators, designers, thinkers, technologists, and environmentally and socially concerned individuals in one of India's most developing cities - Vadodara
